The special issue grew out of the event "(newwindow)Honoring Eve: A Symposium Celebrating the Work of Eve Kosofsky Sedgwick":http://www.bu.edu/phpbin/calendar/event.php?id=92477&cid=73, which was held on October 31, 2009, at Boston University.
