The winter 2010 issue of the <i>Harvard Journal of Law & Gender</i> (Vol. 33:1) is a tribute to Eve Kosofsky Sedgwick, featuring essays by Janet Halley, Katherine M. Franke, Clare Huntington, Susan R. Schmeiser, Philomina Tsoukala, and Darren Rosenblum. This issue was organized by Janet Halley, who is the Royall Professor of Law at Harvard Law School.
